## Contact-form activation
The form uses FormSubmit. After the first test submission, FormSubmit sends an activation email to davidwyoe@gmail.com. Open that email and approve the form endpoint. Future submissions will then be delivered to that address and visitors will be redirected to contact-confirmation.html.
