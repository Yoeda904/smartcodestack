Who We Are

Experienced technology leaders helping the next generation build.

Smart Code Stack was created to connect enterprise-grade technology experience with the speed, creativity, and practical needs of growing businesses.

Smart Code Stack was formed by a private group of experienced technology stakeholders, business leaders, and product innovators who have helped develop, support, and commercialize technology across the modern digital economy.

Our founders have chosen to remain outside the public spotlight. That discretion allows Smart Code Stack to remain focused on its mission: helping promising businesses transform ambitious ideas into practical, scalable, and market-ready technology.

The organization brings together experience in software development, product strategy, digital platforms, business operations, and technology commercialization.

Philanthropic Mission

Backing founders whose ideas can create durable value.

Smart Code Stack believes that access to technology, expertise, and strategic guidance can materially change the trajectory of an emerging business.

Through privately funded initiatives, the organization has supported startups and growing companies across the United States. This support may include financial contributions, software resources, product guidance, mentorship, technical assistance, and strategic counsel.

To respect the privacy of the organizations and individuals involved, Smart Code Stack does not publicly disclose the financial value of its contributions or publish a complete list of participating companies.

Annual Catalyst Award

SureLoadr LLC Named the 2026 Catalyst Award Winner

The Smart Code Stack Catalyst Award is presented once each year to one emerging company whose technology solves a meaningful real-world problem, demonstrates practical market value, and has the potential to create lasting industry impact.

SureLoadr LLC was selected for applying accessible software and freight broker credit intelligence to help owner-operators, small trucking companies, and fleets evaluate payment risk before accepting a load.
