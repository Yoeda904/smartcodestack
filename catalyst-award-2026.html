2026 Winner SureLoadr Smart Code Stack Catalyst Award

About the Winner

Freight broker credit intelligence built for carriers.

SureLoadr LLC is a technology company developing practical credit-intelligence tools for the trucking industry. Its platform is designed to help owner-operators, small trucking companies, and growing fleets evaluate freight brokers before committing equipment, fuel, and time to a load.

By making broker credit information easier to understand and faster to access, SureLoadr helps carriers assess payment risk, review relevant credit indicators, and approach freight decisions with greater confidence.

Why SureLoadr Was Selected

Technology addressing a real operating challenge.

The Catalyst Award recognizes emerging businesses that apply software to meaningful, practical problems. SureLoadr stood out for its clear focus on the day-to-day financial risk faced by trucking carriers.

01

Carrier-Focused Insight

SureLoadr is designed around the needs of carriers evaluating whether a freight broker is likely to pay reliably.

02

Practical Decision Support

The platform translates credit information into a streamlined view intended to support faster, better-informed load decisions.

03

Industry Accessibility

SureLoadr aims to make useful broker intelligence more accessible to owner-operators and smaller fleets that need efficient risk tools.

“SureLoadr represents the kind of focused, practical innovation the Catalyst Award was created to recognize.”

Smart Code Stack congratulates the SureLoadr team and looks forward to seeing its technology continue to develop and serve the trucking community.
